Hi, my name is Vinicius1

I am a programmer and game designer based off Salvador, Bahia. I’m studying game development as an undergrad at the State University of Bahia and my main research interests lie at the intersection between game and web technologies.

Unity3D is my weapon of choice and C# is my main programming language, but I also know little bit of golang, Node.js, and Docker.

I am also disabled. Through a set of unfortunate circumstances involving a motorcycle accident in late 2018, I have sustained elbow injuries that have left me with limited use of my hands and I now interact with the computer using a set of assistive technologies, with the main one being Talon (made by Ryan Hileman, can’t thank him enough!).

You can find me @twitter, @github, or @itch
